About

Vision

In the world of theatre where LGBTQ+ characters are often misrepresented, we believe in shining spotlight on authentic storytelling. Because everyone at center stage deserves to be out and proud.

Mission

We provide a safe, inclusive space where users can:

  • Read articles
  • Watch interviews with LGBTQ+ actors and creators
  • Engage in meaningful discussions about representation,storytelling and more!

Whether you are passionate about Queer theatre or simply eager to learn more. Our community welcomes everyone who values diversity on stage.

Values

  • Authenticity
  • Learning
  • Accessibility
  • Inclusivity
