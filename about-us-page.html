About Us

We are Out N Center, Your theatre-focused social hub for updates and discussions on live LGBTQ+ productions in the Europe.

Our Story

Theatre is a reflection of life, a space where stories come alive, voices are heard, and perspectives are shared. But for too long, outdated stereotypes have limited the depth and diversity of the narratives we see on stage. It’s time to break free. Out N Center aims to support theatre productions that spark change and truly represent everyone.

Our Vision

In a world where the unfamiliar often spark fear, we believe that true understanding comes from getting to know one another, because to be truly human, we all need compassion and love.

Our Mission

Our brand is a dedicated platform for theater lovers to explore, discuss, and celebrate LGBTQ+ representation in theatre.

We provide a safe, inclusive space where users can read articles, watch interviews with LGBTQ+ actors and creators, and engage in meaningful discussions about representation, storytelling and more. Whether you are passionate about queer theater or simply eager to learn more, our community welcomes everyone who values diversity on stage.
